About Us

Khula Systems: Closing the Loop on technology and Agriculture.

At Khula Systems, we believe in a world where technology and nature work in harmony. Founded in 2025, we exist to solve two of Africa's pressing challenges: the growing mountain of electronic waste and the urgent need for sustainable food security.

We see opportunity where others see obsolescence. We are building Africa's first circular economy ecosystem, connecting the dots between urban digital waste and rural agricultural prosperity.

We achieve this through two interconnected divisions:

Khula Intelligence(AgTech)

We are building a future where every farmer has access to the same intelligence as a large-scale agribusiness. By leveraging our expertise, we are developing aAI-driven platform for precision agriculture . Our goal is to empower organic farmers to maximize yield with minimal input, making sustainable farming profitable and accessible across the continent

From secure data dstruction to smart farming - Khula Systems is engineering a more sustainable and prosperous African future.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading provider of sustainable technology solutions by closing the loop between the "Khula Model": We recover value from the past (e-waste) to build the digital tools of the future.
